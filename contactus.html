Meet The Team

Jordan "Jae" Ramirez

Jae is the co-founder and CEO of Nomad Skate Co. With a passion for skateboarding and a commitment to inclusivity, Jae leads the company with the goal of making skate culture open to everyone, no matter their background.

Avery Tran

Avery is the creative mind behind Nomad Skate Co’s designs. They believe skateboarding should be an expression of freedom and individuality, and they work to make sure everyone’s voice is represented in the brand’s style and products.

Brandon "B" Nguyen

B handles community outreach and events for Nomad Skate Co. They’re dedicated to building a welcoming space for all skaters, encouraging freedom of expression, and ensuring that everyone has a place in the skateboarding world.
